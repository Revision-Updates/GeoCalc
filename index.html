GeoCalc

Mathematical geometry solver, including built in linear equations for area, volume and surface area. Produced for TI Basic programmable graphical calculators, designed specifically for Casio variant.

View project on GitHub

Welcome to GeoCalc Repository.

GeoCalc - a programmer's friend

Supported Features:

Area of Geons:
  • Square
  • Rectangle
  • Parallelogram
  • Trapezoid
  • Circle
  • Ellipse
  • Triangle
  • Regular Polygon
Volume of Geons:
  • Cube
  • Rectangular Prism
  • Irregular Prism
  • Cylinder
  • Pyramid
  • Cone
  • Sphere
  • Ellipsoid
Surface Area of Geons:
  • Cube
  • Prism(s) (both Lateral and Total equations supported)
  • Sphere
